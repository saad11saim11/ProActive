About ProActive

It Solutions

Established in 2017 on the island of Aruba, we have been enabling and protecting our clients with innovative and high-quality technology solutions ever since. Our robust solutions stacks, aka "ProCloud Bundles", can help your business succeed in today's modern workplace. Whether you have 5 users or 150 users, our solutions are scalable and secure with predictable costs, making it easy for you to budget for your IT needs.

Innovative Technology & Solutions For Your Business That Just Works.

Our Managed Services give you peace of mind when it comes to your IT needs!

With the ever-evolving technology landscape, it is more important than ever that IT environments have constant protection and attention. However, many organizations just like yours are quickly overwhelmed by the sheer volume of business demands required by their IT systems around the clock. This is where Proactive IT Solutions Managed Services comes in. With years of experience and strategic local and international technology partnerships, Proactive IT Solutions can provide you with a vast array of security-oriented services and products.

Why Proactive IT Solutions:

  • Easy to reach helpdesk

  • Secure & reliable IT infrastructure

  • Work in the cloud or in a hybrid environment

  • Protection from modern attacks

  • Increased productivity for your employees

  • Less downtime of your network & devices

It is estimated that over 80% of today's successful attacks happen due to user error. Don't let careless mistakes and untrained users be the gateway for attackers to your network and valuable data. A breach can significantly disturb your business operations with costly downtime. With active and preventive system monitoring and remediation, we can significantly reduce your cyber attack surface, helping you reduce or entirely prevent these embarrassing and costly situations.

Contact us today for your free no strings attached consultation.
